About Our Trust

Learn about our mission, vision, and commitment to educational excellence

Our Story

JAY HIRAJI BABA SHAIKSHNIK V BHUUDDESHIY SANSTHA was established with a noble vision to provide free quality education to underprivileged children in rural and backward areas. Founded by Dhramraj Padvi, our trust has been serving the community for over 15 years, transforming lives through education.

We believe that education is the fundamental right of every child, regardless of their economic background. Our trust works tirelessly to ensure that no child is left behind due to financial constraints.

Our Mission

To provide free, quality education to underprivileged children from LKG to 12th standard, and promote agricultural studies at the graduation level, ensuring holistic development and community upliftment.

Our Vision

To create an educated, empowered, and self-reliant society where every child has access to quality education and opportunities for growth, regardless of their economic background.

Our Values

Integrity, Excellence, Compassion, Community Service, and Sustainable Development are the core values that guide our every action and decision.

Our Objectives

Provide free education to children from economically backward families

Promote agricultural education and sustainable farming practices

Ensure holistic development through academic and extracurricular activities

Create awareness about the importance of education in rural areas

Support skill development and vocational training programs

Foster community participation and social responsibility

Our Impact

Transforming Lives Through Education

Over the years, our trust has made a significant impact on the lives of hundreds of children and their families. We have successfully:

  • Provided free education to over 500 students
  • Graduated 50+ students from our agricultural programs
  • Improved literacy rates in rural communities
  • Created employment opportunities through skill development
  • Established sustainable farming practices in the region
