Our Educational Programs

Comprehensive education from early childhood to graduation level

School Education (LKG to 12th Standard)

Pre-Primary (LKG & UKG)

Foundation years focusing on basic skills, social development, and creative learning through play-based activities.

  • Basic language and numeracy skills
  • Social and emotional development
  • Creative arts and crafts
  • Physical activities and games

Primary Education (1st to 5th)

Comprehensive primary education with focus on core subjects and character building.

  • Mathematics, Science, and Social Studies
  • English and Hindi language skills
  • Computer literacy
  • Sports and physical education

Middle School (6th to 8th)

Advanced learning with specialized subjects and skill development programs.

  • Advanced mathematics and sciences
  • Literature and languages
  • Computer applications
  • Life skills and values education

Secondary Education (9th to 12th)

Specialized streams preparing students for higher education and career paths.

  • Science, Commerce, and Arts streams
  • Career counseling and guidance
  • Competitive exam preparation
  • Leadership and personality development

Agricultural Studies (Graduation Level)

Bachelor of Science in Agriculture

Our specialized agricultural program is designed to promote sustainable farming practices and modern agricultural techniques. This program equips students with practical knowledge and skills needed for the agricultural sector.

Program Highlights:

  • Duration: 4 years (8 semesters)
  • Eligibility: 12th standard with Science background
  • Mode: Full-time residential program
  • Fees: Completely free for eligible students

Agricultural Course Structure

Semester 1-2 (Foundation)

  • Basic Agricultural Sciences
  • Soil Science and Management
  • Plant Physiology
  • Agricultural Mathematics
  • English Communication

Semester 3-4 (Core Subjects)

  • Crop Production Technology
  • Agricultural Engineering
  • Plant Pathology
  • Agricultural Economics
  • Farm Management

Semester 5-6 (Specialization)

  • Horticulture and Floriculture
  • Animal Husbandry
  • Agricultural Extension
  • Organic Farming
  • Agricultural Marketing

Semester 7-8 (Advanced)

  • Precision Agriculture
  • Sustainable Development
  • Research Methodology
  • Project Work
  • Internship
