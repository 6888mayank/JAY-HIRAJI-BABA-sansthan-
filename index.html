Message from Our Founder

Dhramraj Padvi

"Education is the most powerful weapon which you can use to change the world. At JAY HIRAJI BABA SHAIKSHNIK V BHUUDDESHIY SANSTHA, we believe that every child, regardless of their economic background, deserves access to quality education. We are committed to providing free education to underprivileged children and promoting agricultural studies to ensure food security and sustainable development. Together, we can build a brighter future for our community."
