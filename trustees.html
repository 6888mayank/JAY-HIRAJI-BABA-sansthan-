Our Trustees

Meet the dedicated leaders who guide our educational mission

Founder & Chairman

Dhramraj Padvi

Visionary Leader & Social Reformer

Dhramraj Padvi is the visionary founder of JAY HIRAJI BABA SHAIKSHNIK V BHUUDDESHIY SANSTHA. With over two decades of experience in social work and education, he has dedicated his life to uplifting underprivileged communities through education.

Key Achievements:

  • Established the trust in 2008 with a vision for free education
  • Successfully provided education to over 500 underprivileged children
  • Pioneered agricultural education programs in rural areas
  • Received multiple awards for social service and education
  • Actively involved in community development initiatives
"Education is not just about acquiring knowledge; it's about transforming lives and building a better society. Every child deserves the opportunity to learn and grow."

Message from Our Trustees

"As trustees of JAY HIRAJI BABA SHAIKSHNIK V BHUUDDESHIY SANSTHA, we are committed to the noble cause of providing free, quality education to underprivileged children. Our collective vision is to create an educated, empowered, and self-reliant society where every child has the opportunity to realize their full potential.

We believe that education is the most powerful tool for social transformation. Through our dedicated efforts and the support of our community, we have been able to make a significant impact on the lives of hundreds of children and their families.

We invite you to join us in this mission of educational empowerment and community development. Together, we can build a brighter future for generations to come."
